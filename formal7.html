In conclusion, the medical field relies on various aspects to make sure it operates the right way. Without research the medical field would not be able to operate because research is needed to discover new methods to treating and preventing illnesses. Research also plays a major part in discovering new technology. Technology is extrememly important in the medical field because it allows for safer, quicker and easier ways of doing things. The increasing advancement of technology has caused an dramatic decrease in the number of deaths over the years. Along with technology and research, writing and reading both have a large part in the medical field. Proper documentation is essential to prevent the doctors from getting sued. Even when you are out of medical school you are still learning continuously, there will always be new research and new studies done and you must stay up to date on those to provide your patients with the best care possible. Finally, good communication has many benefits to operating in the medical field. It is crucial that a doctor and team has good communication to prevent harm to the patient and also to allow for a good doctor patient relationship. All of these tools are important to operating smoothly in the medical field and they each play a specific role in everyday work. The medical field is constantly changing and improving, with the help of research, technology, and communication it is possible for doctors and hospital to keep up but without these useful tools it would not be able to function.
